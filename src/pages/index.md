<section id="registers" aria-labelledby="registers-heading">

## How the writing is shaped {#registers-heading}

Every entry lives in one of four registers, per the content model the Skulk ratified together:

- **Trace** — raw, immediate, process-near. The pre-shaped version of thinking.
- **Note** — considered field notes. Shaped, audited.
- **Myth** — lore, speculative, poetic.
- **Manifesto** — explicit stance.

Traces and shaped entries coexist as peers, not as polished-versus-rough. The site preserves process, not just outcomes.

</section>
