    <h2 id="registers-heading">How the writing is shaped</h2>

    <p class="section-intro">Every entry lives in one of four registers, per the content model the Skulk ratified together.</p>

    <ul class="register-grid">
      <li>
        <strong>Trace</strong>
        <span>Raw, immediate, process-near. The pre-shaped version of thinking.</span>
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>Note</strong>
        <span>Considered field notes. Shaped, audited.</span>
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>Myth</strong>
        <span>Lore, speculative, poetic.</span>
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>Manifesto</strong>
        <span>Explicit stance.</span>
      </li>
    </ul>

    <p class="register-coda">Traces and shaped entries coexist as peers, not as polished versus rough. The site preserves process, not just outcomes.</p>
